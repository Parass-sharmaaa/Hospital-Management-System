About Us

Welcome to Thapar Health Centre, your trusted partner in healthcare and wellness located at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology in Patiala. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by a team of dedicated healthcare professionals committed to providing exceptional medical services.

Dr. Mahesh Sharma, Faculty In-charge, T.I.E.T, Patiala.

Our Mission is to enhance the health and well-being of our students, faculty, and staff through comprehensive medical care, preventive services, and wellness education.

Health Centre day timings are:
  • Summer: 8:30 AM - 7:30 PM
  • Winter: 8:30 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Night Hours: 10:00 PM - 6:00 AM

    • Ambulance service available 24x7.

    At Thapar Health Centre, we believe in a holistic approach to health and wellness. Our goal is to ensure that every member of the Thapar community has access to quality healthcare services in a welcoming and supportive environment.
